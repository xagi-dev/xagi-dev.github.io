What is it?

We dream of a world where highly general RL agents and humans work in co-existance

But humans suck at saying what they want, they can't really create perfectly crafted reward-functions which RL-agents can consumer, at best humans can provide some feedback

We aim to build and deploy useful RL-agents which work on human-feedback

As a zeroeth-step we started on building a highly general terminal-assistant
