What is it?

We dream of a world where highly general RL agents and humans co-exist

But humans suck at expressing what they want,

Humans can't create perfect reward-functions which RL-agents can consume

To circumvent this we aim to build and deploy useful RL-agents which work on human-feedback

As a zeroeth-step we started on building a highly general terminal-assistant with natural-language-interface
